Other Interesting Civil Engineering Research Topics

   

  • Ground Motion Modeling - The ability to model predict how the ground moves during the event of an earthquake can help engineers design for more resilient structures, and limit damage as a result of these natural disasters
  • Advanced Sensors and Intelligent Systems - Integrating sensors into structure that can identify and respond to defects within a strucure before disaster happens
  • Sampling for Environmental Monitoring - Developing efficient and accurate ways to measure the amount of pollutants within our environment is the first step in understanding the problem of climate change and the steps we need to take to solve it.
