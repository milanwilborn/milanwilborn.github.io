What is Bamboo? 
Wondering what exactly bamboo is and why it is so important? Here are some key facts:
  1. Contrary to popular belief, bamboo is actually a species of grass rather than a type of tree
  2. Bamboo is one of the fastest growing plants in the world, and is found on 5 of the 7 continents: South America, North America, Asia, Africa, and Oceania
  3. Bamboo's strength rivals that of both concrete and steel.
  4. EXTRA INTERESTING FACT: Bamboo was the only plant to survive the radiation from the atomic bombs dropped in Hiroshima
